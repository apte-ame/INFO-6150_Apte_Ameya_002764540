About Us

Being a Northeastern University's "Tinkering Labs" initiative, our goal lies in providing affordable and customizable food boxes to university students.

With this motive, our founder introduced the first "Choose It Yourself" Food Box for the students at a subsidized rate in collaboration with Northeastern University.

Our motive is to not only provide food boxes with all the necessary items, but to also provide customization and affordable options to all the students such that a student saves a lot of time not thinking about what to eat and studies well along with having healthy meal options.

Currently serving only Northeastern University, we are working on expanding our business to nearby universities over the Boston area pretty soon!
